Features

Everything your team needs

Built for individuals, families, teams, and communities of any size.

Direct Messages
Lightning-fast 1:1 and group messaging with read receipts and reactions.
Crystal-Clear Calls
HD voice and video calls for up to 1,000 participants. No time limits, ever.
Spaces & Channels
Organize conversations by topic, project, or team. Threads keep things focused.
End-to-End Encryption
