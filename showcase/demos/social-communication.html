Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Commune brings together private messaging and community spaces in one beautiful, secure platform.

End-to-End Encrypted
Every message, photo, and call is protected with military-grade encryption. Only you and your recipient can read them.
Group Spaces
Create private groups or open communities. Organize conversations with channels, threads, and roles.
Voice & Video
Crystal-clear calls for two or twenty. Encrypted video rooms with screen sharing, reactions, and spatial audio.
Shared Albums
A private photo library for every conversation. Photos are end-to-end encrypted and never analyzed by AI.
Reactions & Threads
Keep conversations tidy. React to any message and spin up focused threads without cluttering the main chat.
Cross-Platform
iOS, Android, Mac, and web. Your messages arrive instantly, with a consistent experience on every device you own.
